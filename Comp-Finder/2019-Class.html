Score Methodology

- Long Term (LT) score represents my QB model for predicting career NFL efficiency (TANY/A*). LT is based on college TANY/A*, college Rush%, age, and draft postion.

- Rookie Readiness (RR) score represents my model for predicting efficiency in year one. Being efficient in college, mobile, and older is important to RR.

- LT is more important in general for evaluating QB prospects, but RR can help add context if you are looking for a QB ready to start now, for your fantasy team or favorite NFL team.

- Learn more about TANY/A* and my QB prospect models over at TwoQBs, or give me a shout on twitter if you have questions, suggestions, or just want to discuss QB prospects.
